👋 Hi there, I'm Luna Tian

📰 I'm a citizen journalist, wanderer, and storyteller currently based in Sweden. I study media, democracy, and human rights, and write about the things we often forget—or are forced to forget.

✍️ This blog documents stories from the margins: about digital surveillance, censorship, exile, identity, and quiet resistance in China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.

🌱 I believe in memory. In witness. In speaking — even when it's hard.
